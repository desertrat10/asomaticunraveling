# Project Funding

*A Somatic Unraveling* runs in phases, and each phase funds differently. This document explains the structure — partly so supporters know what they're funding, partly because a project about patient sovereignty should be transparent about its economics.

**The rule underneath it all:** the body sets the pace, so the funding can never depend on deadlines. Nothing here promises a delivery date.

---

## The phase structure

| Phase | Window | What happens | How it's funded |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — Experimental** | Mar 2026 → 2027/28 | Daily field notes + structured recalibration program (traditional + holistic modalities, all logged through the healingOS) | Self-funded layer: subscriptions, tips, workshops, client work |
| **2 — Writing** | ~12 months after Phase 1 | Fresh manuscript composition from the field-note record | Fellowships + grants (applications begin fall 2026) |
| **3 — Publishing** | Overlaps late Phase 2 | Proposal, agent, publisher | Stands on the finished body of work |

Grant applications always run one phase ahead: Phase 2 money gets applied for during Phase 1, because fellowship cycles take 9–12 months. The self-funded layer covers the phase currently underway.

## Phase 1 — how to support right now

The experimental phase is funded directly by readers and by my own work. Support options live at [signalsandseeds.xyz](https://signalsandseeds.xyz/):

- **Patron ($12/mo)** — ongoing support for everything produced on the site.
- **The Inner Signal ($55/mo)** — a monthly live group conversation on AI, communications, sovereignty, and regenerative economics, plus early access to CommsOS frameworks.
- **One-time contribution** — [a single gift in any amount](https://signalsandseeds.xyz/#/portal/support), for those who'd rather give once than subscribe. Contributors are acknowledged in the book.

What this money buys: the treatment program (PT, yoga therapy, cranial sacral — mostly out-of-pocket), the time to keep the daily practice daily, and the infrastructure you're reading right now.

**Funding a full phase (tax-deductible).** I'm co-founder and president of the Factland Foundation, a 501(c)(3). If you'd like to fund an entire phase of this project as a tax-deductible donation, that's a conversation I'd welcome: **cstreet@factland.org**

## Phase 2 — the grant layer

The project sits at an unusual intersection — literary, medical-humanities, and open-data — which qualifies it for several funding worlds at once. Applications begin fall 2026 for the writing year. The open-data companion (this repo, the healingOS methodology, the eventual decentralized archive) has its own funding track in the public-goods ecosystem.

Funders and fellowship programs interested in the project: **cstreet@factland.org**

## What funding does not buy

Editorial influence over the manuscript, access to the raw field notes, or a schedule. The book arrives when the skeleton is whole. Sponsorship is witnessing, not commissioning.
