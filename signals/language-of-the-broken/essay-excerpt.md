# The Language of the Broken

**Published on [Signals & Seeds](https://signalsandseeds.xyz/the-language-of-the-broken/)** — 2026

> *Excerpt. Read the full essay on [Signals & Seeds →](https://signalsandseeds.xyz/the-language-of-the-broken/)*

---

My current somatic journey is disrupting most aspects of my life at this moment. One massive
disruption for this word nerd is kind of obvious given that my entire world revolves around
communicating narratives. Language is now both a barrier and a trigger. I lack access to
adequate language for the new floor I'm forced to build.

Words like "recovery, healing, cure, restore, normal" no longer apply; but little exists to
replace them. The friction is subtle at first but steadily grows into an uncontrollable flame
as well-intended humans in your sphere use the Language of the Able to try and give hope or
advice. After a while, the Language of the Able just reinforces the fact that my floor is now
crooked, or non-existent, and we no longer stand on even, equal ground.

And the language I am learning to use, and also learning to reject, is one of the hardest
aspects of building my new floor.

- Not recovery — re-calibration.
- Not healing — maintaining.
- Not normal — "my new floor."
- Not restoration — acceptance.
